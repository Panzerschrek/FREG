23 Items:
24  - Air: passable, transparent. Default block.
25  - Grass
26  - Stone
27  - Chicken: walking chiken. Makes sounds. Burns in a fire.
28  - Fire. Burns things as chickens and piles. Dissapears in one hour.
29  - Chest. Can contain up to 30 items. Collects items falling on it.
30  - Pile. Can contain up to 30 items. Collects items falling on it.
31  Dissapears after 24 game hours.
32  - Clock: unstackable. If there is a clock in an inventory, exact time is shown instead
33  of part of a day.
34  - Compass: unstackable. If there is a compass in an inventory, direction is shown.
35  - Iron ingot: iron armor (helmet, chestplate, greaves, boots) can be crafted from it.
36  - Iron helmet: unstackable. Used as a helmet. Can be crafted from 5 iron ingots.
37  - Iron boots: unstackable. Can be crafted from 4 iron ingots.
38  - Iron greaves: unstackable. Can be crafted from 7 iron ingots.
39  - Iron chestplate: unstackable. Can be crafted from 8 iron ingots.
